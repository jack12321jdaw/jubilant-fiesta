About Us

We are the first student-led CCA at Malvern School. Every Wednesday from 3:20 PM to 4:30 PM, we gather to organize and participate in cultural activities to share the charm of Chinese culture.

We encourage all students interested in Chinese culture to join our club, regardless of nationality. Everyone is welcome to participate!

Activity Schedule

We hold activities every Wednesday from 3:20 PM to 4:30 PM. All students interested in Chinese culture are welcome to attend.

Our Members

Currently, our club has 10 members, a diverse team made up of students from different countries.

Chinese Leader: Chen Ziyang

Chen Ziyang is our Chinese leader, responsible for organizing and leading the club's activities, helping everyone better understand and experience Chinese culture.

Japanese Representative: Ryuga

Ryuga is our Japanese representative, responsible for communicating and liaising with Japanese students, helping bridge the cultural differences and connections between China and Japan.
